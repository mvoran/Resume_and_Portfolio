Stinky Spoke 2017
Here is a simple POC of a speed map for bike rides. I've seen plenty of tools that plot ride data on a map. What I haven't seen are tools that map ride speeds.

I think this would be most useful as a tool to evaluate total ride performance and then identify segments of a ride where performance could be improved (e.g., climbs, descents).

For reference, anything under 3.0 mph is deep red. Anything over 18.0 mph is deep blue.
